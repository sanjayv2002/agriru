About Dr.Agriru

Dr.Agriru which intends to assist the young planters in making an informed decision about which crop to grow depending on the sowing season, obtained soil characteristics and his farm’s geographical location It recommends the perfect crops based on the condition of soil and using the other external climatic factors. It does the detailed analysis only based on Indian soil and climatic conditions. And it’s User-friendly interface provides all users to utilize all the resources in the software. We have used data sets from multiple sources which helps in solving the non-linear problems. The solution will benefit farmers to maximize productivity in agriculture, reduce soil degradation in cultivated fields, and reduce fertilizer use in crop production by recommending the right crop by considering various attributes.
